About Us

CodeHerat is a youth-led initiative based in Herat,Afghanistan,focused on empowering Afghan girls through STEM(Science,Tenchnology,Engineering,and Mathmatics).Our mission is to provide grils with access to quality educationin coding,web development,and digital literacy.We believe that that by equopping girls with 21st-century skills,we can build a brighter,more inclusivefuture for Afghanistan.Our workshops are designed to be practical,interactive,and inspiring-helping girls gain confidence,creativity,and problem-solving abilities.
